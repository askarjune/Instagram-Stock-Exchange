About this project:

What did we make this with?

Inst@ck is built using HTML/CSS/Javascript and Bootstrap for the front-end and Python for the backend. We also used text mining to gather the follower count on Instagram every 7 seconds. Then, we used the c3js chart library to visualize the data on our website.

Why did we make this?

We wanted to make an application to make learning about the stock market fun and relevant for children and teenagers. We are all sophomore CS students from Wellesley College.

Who made this?

Inst@ck Exchange was created at Hack@Smith 2017 by Evelyn Chen, Maxine Hood, Amber Skarjune, and Alice Li. We are all sophomore CS students from Wellesley College.
