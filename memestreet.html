About Stocks

What's a Stock Exchange?

A stock exchange is a market in which securities are bought and sold. In this case "securities" are stocks in an Instagram celebrity.

How do I get coins?

To gain coins, buy stocks in a celebrity you think will be gaining more followers. Your goal is to sell your stock for a higher price than you bought it for to make a profit.

Here's a tip!

Invest in stocks with less followers but a high growth rate. If they continue rising, the stock will lead to high PROFIT$.
